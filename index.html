Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept mobile payments - M-pesa, Tigo Pesa, T-pesa, HaloPesa, etc (Pre-paid).

Can I get a refund after purchasing an item?

No, you cannot get a refund after purchasing an item.

How do I get my product after purchasing it?

You can choose to self pickup at our pickup point or request for a delivery service which we charge according to a place.

Do you accept cash on delivery?

Yes, we do accept cash on delivery.

Where is your shop located?

We don't have a physical shop yet, we still are an online business. But our pickup point is at Masaki, Dar es Salaam.

What is your opening time?

Our services are available from 9:00am - 4:00pm,
Mondays - Saturdays.

Do you do delivery services?

Yes, we do delivery services only in Dar es Salaam but it is NOT FREE. It is on the customer's expense.

Do you sell wholesale packages?

No, you can only purchase in retail.
